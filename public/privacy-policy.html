Our Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23rd October 2020

  • Your privacy is important to us. It is ACM JUIT's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, ACM Omnia.
  • We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

  • You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.

01.Some FAQs

Q1. What is ACM ?
A1. The Association for Computing Machinery is a US-based international learned society for computing. It was founded in 1947, and is the world's largest scientific and educational computing society.

Q2. What does ACM do ?
A2. The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges.
